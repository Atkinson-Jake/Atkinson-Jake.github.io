The ultimate hunting destination for those seeking a unique combination of premier whitetail trophies, family-friendly hunting experiences, and devoted guides that will work hard to deliver a flawless hunt.

Inquire About a Hunt Today!

At Twin Creeks Ranch, we welcome hunters of all ages and skill-levels to join us for a one-of-a-kind whitetail hunting experience. As a family owned and operated business, we have always encouraged families and especially females to join the hunting community. Our founder, Audrey Murtland, was the first ever recipient of the Safari Club International "Diana Award" that honors female hunters who have excelled in international big game hunting and who have spent their lives working to ehnance wildlife conservation and education. She is an inspiration for our business, and we hope that you will get a chance to meet her when you bring your friends and family on an amazing whitetail hunt with us!
